About Me

I am a Frontend Developer and Systems Engineer with 3 years of experience, skilled in a variety of technologies including React, Redux, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and Firebase.

My background includes not only solid technical expertise, but also strong interpersonal skills and teamwork experience. I am self-taught, sociable, and strategic, always eager to support my co-workers and maintain a high level of activity within my projects.

Fluent in both Spanish and Portuguese, I am able to effectively work and communicate in diverse environments. My objective is to deliver top-quality digital solutions for clients, focusing on both exceptional visual design and high productivity.

Currently residing in Bogota, Colombia, I hold a degree in Systems Engineering from EAN University.
